Welcome to Keepr Notes

Timestamped notes for YouTube that live on your own disk.

  1. 1 · Open any YouTube video

    Keepr adds a small note button to the player controls.

  2. 2 · Capture the exact moment

    Click the player button or press Alt+N. The current second is saved instantly.

  3. 3 · Open the panel and write

    Click the Keepr icon in the toolbar. Click any note to jump back. Export to Markdown or back up anytime.
